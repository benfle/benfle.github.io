How I Use My Notebook

#

I use a Federated Wiki to make a network of the models (concepts, ideas, patterns...) that I come across.

#

When reading books, I don't keep exhaustive notes. That would be too time-consuming. I read the book in its entirety while highlighting and taking small notes on the book itself.

#

I come back to the book after a few days to figure out what were the main ideas and how to fit them in my existing network of concepts.

#

Each book will have a page summarizing the main ideas and linking to related concepts. Good books add concepts or expand on existing concepts. The process is additive.
