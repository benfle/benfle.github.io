Runnable Models

#

It is important that the models be small and runnable so that the designer can experiment with different design decisions.

#

A classic example of a runnable model is a LISP evaluator. The fourth chapter of "Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs" explores various design decisions for this evaluator.
