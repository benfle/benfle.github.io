Benoît Fleury - Painting

I started oil painting in August 2018 thanks to Mark Carder's Draw Mix Paint method.

I made my own easel and shadow box as explained by Mark on his website. I used the Geneva Fine Art studio palette and essential set of colors: cadmium yellow, french ultramarine, titanium white, burnt umber, and pyrole red.

I followed Mark's method closely for the first paintings: drawing with the proportional divider, mixing the colors first, and constantly checking colors. I took more liberties later. First by not pre-mixing the colors, then by checking the colors less. After a year, I abandonned the drawing part and directly drew the shadows.
