Habituation

#

Habituation is the process of getting used to a situation. It is a kind of abstraction process. It is great for learning.

#

But we also often get used to situations that are problematic. It is the job of the designer to notice those misfits and fix them.

#

See Tony Fadell talk about Habituation at TED2015.
