Bohm Dialogue

#

Because we are trap in our own thoughts and mental models, we need dialogue with others to observe our own thinking.

#

In The Fifth Discipline, Peter Senge recommends Bohm dialogues to help a team to learn together.

#

The three conditions necessary for dialogues:

#

  1. suspend our assumptions
  2. regard one another as equals
  3. a "facilitator holds the context"
