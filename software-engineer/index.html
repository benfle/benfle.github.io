Software Engineer

#

Most of my work has been at the intersection of software design and software engineering. I believe that the design process is fundamentally iterative, requiring constant back and forth between the analysis of the problem and the synthesis of a solution. My research agenda provides more details about my views on computer science and software engineering.

#

My most recent projects involved Clojure, Datomic, JavaScript, and Amazon Web Services (AWS). However, while I enjoy working with good tools, I believe that a focus on design, the software development process and the culture of the organization have a bigger impact on the success of a project.

#

I live in Tallahassee, Florida (US Eastern Time, GMT-5) and not planning to relocate.

#
